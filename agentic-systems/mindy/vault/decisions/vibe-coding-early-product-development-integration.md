# Vibe Coding Integration in Early Product Development

*Decision made: 2026-04-21*

## Context
Team needed enhanced collaboration and iterative design capabilities in early product development processes for improved team feedback and board meeting deliverables.

## Decision
Integrate vibe coding into early product development processes:
- Focus on proactive team feedback and iterative design for board meeting presentations
- Emphasize prototyping for progress demonstration rather than finalized implementation
- Integrate collaborative design sessions with development workflow
- Support rapid iteration and feedback cycles for product validation
- Enhance cross-functional collaboration between design, product, and engineering

## Rationale
- Improves collaboration through early feedback and iterative design processes
- Enables rapid prototyping for stakeholder communication and validation
- Supports board meeting preparation with progress demonstration focus
- Enhances cross-functional coordination and communication effectiveness
- Facilitates faster iteration cycles and product validation processes
- Aligns development approach with startup agility and stakeholder communication needs

## Implementation
- Sunny leading vibe coding integration efforts for board meeting deliverables
- FigmaMake usage for interface development ahead of coding implementation
- Collaborative design sessions integration with development workflow
- Prototype focus for board meeting presentation rather than final implementation

## Status
Active implementation
