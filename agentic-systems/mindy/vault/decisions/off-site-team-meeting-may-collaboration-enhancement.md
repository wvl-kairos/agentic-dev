# Off-Site Team Meeting for May Collaboration Enhancement

*Decision made: 2026-04-28*

## Context
Team recognized need for enhanced in-person collaboration to address complex manufacturing project requirements and improve team dynamics after period of remote work coordination challenges.

## Decision
Plan comprehensive team off-site meeting for May focusing on collaborative development:
- Target locations including Mexico City, Brazil, or Costa Rica for team accessibility
- In-person collaboration session to boost team dynamics and project coordination
- Family attendance encouraged for team members to improve participation
- Focus on complex project work that benefits from intensive collaboration
- Timing selected to avoid high travel costs and team member availability conflicts

## Rationale
- Enhances team collaboration and communication for complex manufacturing system development
- Builds stronger working relationships through face-to-face interaction and coordination
- Provides opportunity for intensive collaboration and strategic planning sessions
- Cost-effective timing outside peak travel periods with better team availability
- Supports team culture development and improved coordination for distributed team
- Addresses geographical challenges with team members across Brazil, Argentina, and Chile

## Implementation
- Team coordination on location selection and logistics planning
- Family-friendly approach to encourage maximum team participation
- Focus on collaborative working sessions and strategic project planning
- Integration with ongoing project timelines and delivery requirements

## Status
In planning with location and timing coordination
