# Meeting Intelligence Platform Implementation

*Decision made: 2026-04-20*

## Context
Team needed comprehensive meeting intelligence capabilities for automated summarization, knowledge extraction, and workflow integration to enhance productivity and information sharing.

## Decision
Implement end-to-end meeting intelligence platform with comprehensive automation:
- Auto-post per-meeting summaries to venture Slack channels
- Venture auto-tagging via Fireflies API for organized content
- Venture-scoped post-meeting Q&A through Mindy integration
- Sensitivity filtering for meeting transcripts to protect confidential information
- Automated workflow from meeting capture through analysis to distribution

## Rationale
- Eliminates manual meeting summary creation and distribution overhead
- Improves knowledge sharing and information accessibility across teams
- Enables automated content organization through intelligent tagging
- Provides Q&A capabilities for meeting content exploration
- Protects sensitive information through automated filtering
- Enhances team productivity through automated meeting intelligence

## Implementation
- Armando completed 4-ticket milestone delivering comprehensive platform capabilities
- Fireflies API integration for automated meeting capture and processing
- Slack integration for automated summary distribution
- Mindy integration for Q&A capabilities and knowledge extraction
- Sensitivity filtering implementation for confidentiality protection

## Status
Implemented and operational
