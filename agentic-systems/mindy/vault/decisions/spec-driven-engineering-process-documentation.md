# Spec-Driven Engineering Process Documentation

*Decision made: 2026-04-21*

## Context
Team needed systematic approach to feature development with clear specifications, Linear integration, and consistent engineering methodology for complex product development.

## Decision
Implement comprehensive spec-driven engineering process:
- Standardized specification documentation requirements
- Linear task organization with clear acceptance criteria
- Integration of design review and technical validation stages
- Systematic approach to feature decomposition and estimation
- Documentation of engineering workflow and best practices

## Rationale
- Ensures consistent approach to feature development across team
- Improves coordination between design, product, and engineering
- Reduces ambiguity and rework through clear specifications
- Supports systematic estimation and planning processes
- Provides framework for quality assurance and validation
- Enables better tracking and accountability for deliverables

## Implementation
- Luis coordinated process documentation creation
- Team review and feedback incorporation on methodology
- Linear project organization aligned with spec-driven approach
- Integration with existing design review and QA workflows
- Training and adoption across engineering team

## Status
Documented and under team review
