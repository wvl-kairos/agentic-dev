# Noise Reduction Model Breakthrough Achievement

*Decision made: 2026-04-22*

## Context
Team needed significant improvement in schedule stability with ATA changes creating substantial operational disruption and uncertainty in production planning and customer communication.

## Decision
Implement and optimize noise reduction model achieving breakthrough performance improvement:
- Deploy model reducing ATA changes from 5,000 to 2,400 (52% improvement)
- Integrate model into monorepo with parameter adjustment capabilities
- Develop chassis delivery prediction model foundation for future exploration
- Advance Bayesian updating framework for arrival date predictions
- Target additional 50% schedule change reduction through rule-set development

## Rationale
- Provides substantial improvement in schedule stability and operational predictability
- Reduces customer communication overhead and operational disruption from frequent changes
- Establishes foundation for advanced predictive modeling and optimization capabilities
- Demonstrates significant ROI through measurable operational improvement metrics
- Enables data-driven schedule optimization and production planning enhancement
- Supports customer confidence through improved delivery prediction accuracy
