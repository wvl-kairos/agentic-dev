# Weekly Incremental Shipping Methodology

*Decision made: 2026-04-28*

## Context
Team needed to establish consistent delivery cadence and improve development velocity while ensuring quality delivery and better coordination across engineering efforts.

## Decision
Implement weekly incremental shipping approach starting with order visibility table:
- Ship components and features weekly rather than large batches
- Begin with order visibility table as foundation for subsequent development
- Emphasize early testing, open communication, and collaborative development
- Focus on backend pagination, sorting, and comprehensive feature delivery
- Coordinate across data, engineering, and product teams for feature completion

## Rationale
- Reduces integration complexity and development cycle bottlenecks
- Enables faster feedback loops and validation cycles for quality improvement
- Supports production readiness through iterative delivery and testing
- Improves team coordination and communication across disciplines
- Aligns with agile development practices and customer validation needs
- Facilitates customer feedback integration through incremental improvements

- Evandro handling comprehensive frontend and backend development for orders table
- Alex supporting data pipeline integration and backend infrastructure
- Team commitment to weekly component delivery with clear coordination processes

## Status
Active implementation with order visibility table as initial focus
