# Technical Decision Filtering Framework

*Decision made: 2026-05-02*

## Context
Antwoine Flowers transitioning from staff data scientist to head of department role required clear framework for technical decision-making to ensure strategic consistency and prevent ad-hoc changes in product engineering direction.

## Decision
Implement technical decision filtering framework through designated technical leads:
- All technical opinions and changes filtered through Alex Maramaldo and Luis Suarez before implementation
- Antwoine focuses on strategic direction and department leadership rather than direct technical decisions
- Alex and Luis serve as technical filters for product engineering and technology direction
- Clear escalation path for technical decisions requiring strategic input
- Maintain strategic oversight while ensuring technical consistency

## Rationale
- Provides clear technical authority and decision-making structure
- Prevents conflicting technical directions and implementation inconsistencies
- Allows Antwoine to focus on strategic leadership and product vision
- Leverages Alex and Luis's technical expertise for consistent engineering decisions
- Supports team scaling and organizational maturity
- Ensures technical decisions align with product engineering mindset

## Implementation
- Alex Maramaldo and Luis Suarez designated as technical decision filters
- Antwoine focusing on strategic leadership and product vision
- Clear communication channels established for technical escalation
- Technical leads coordinate on product engineering direction
- Regular coordination between technical leads and strategic leadership

## Status
Active implementation
