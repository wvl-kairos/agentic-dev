PRODUCT 1: Precision Gear Assembly (prod-gear-assy-100)
============================================================

Description: Three-stage spur gear train for industrial power transmission. Used in conveyor drives, pump systems, and packaging machinery. Output torque rating 450 Nm at 1800 RPM input.
Product Family: Precision Gears
Drawing: DWG-GA-100 Rev D
Standard Lead Time: 18 days
Unit Weight: 12.4 kg

Critical Specifications:
- Gear tooth profile: involute, 20° pressure angle, Module 2.5
- Gear quality: AGMA Class 11 (equivalent to ISO 1328 Grade 5)
- Input shaft bore: 25.000 mm +0.000/-0.013 (CPK target ≥1.33)
- Output shaft runout: ≤0.015 mm TIR
- Surface hardness: 58-62 HRC on tooth flanks (carburized 8620 steel)
- Backlash: 0.05-0.10 mm

PRODUCT 3: Custom Mounting Bracket (prod-brkt-mtg-300)
============================================================

Description: Heavy-duty welded structural bracket for industrial equipment mounting. Customer-specific designs with standard base geometry. Zinc plated per ASTM B633 Type III, Class Fe/Zn 12.
Product Family: Custom Brackets
Drawing: DWG-MB-300 Rev A
Standard Lead Time: 10 days
Unit Weight: 6.2 kg

- Base plate flatness: ≤0.5 mm over 300 mm

- Zinc plating thickness: 12 μm minimum per ASTM B633

NOTES
============================================================

Scrap factors: Gear blanks carry a 3% scrap factor due to heat treat distortion. Hydraulic castings carry 2% for porosity rejects found during pressure test. Bracket steel components carry 1.5% for weld defects. These factors are built into the BOM quantity calculations for MRP planning.

All incoming raw materials are inspected per ACME quality procedure QP-INC-001. Steel certifications (mill certs) are verified against material spec requirements. Components from (supp-precision-steel) have historically maintained a 97.2% OTD rate with zero quality rejections in the last 12 months. Components from (supp-ohio-castings) require 100% incoming inspection due to historical porosity issues (see supplier audit report).
