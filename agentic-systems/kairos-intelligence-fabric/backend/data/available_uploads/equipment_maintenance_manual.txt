============================================================
2. PM SCHEDULES — CNC LATHES (equip-cnc-lathe-01), (equip-cnc-lathe-02)
============================================================

DAILY: Check coolant level (8-12% concentration), chip conveyor, chuck jaw wear, hydraulic pressure (45-55 bar).
WEEKLY: Lubricate guideways (Mobil Vactra No. 2, 200 mL), inspect spindle belt tension (max 12 mm deflection), tailstock runout check (max 0.008 mm), coolant filter replacement if ΔP >0.5 bar.
MONTHLY: Spindle vibration analysis — baseline 2.1 mm/s for (equip-cnc-lathe-01), 1.9 mm/s for (equip-cnc-lathe-02). Alert: 4.0 mm/s. Alarm: 7.0 mm/s (immediate shutdown). Turret bolt torque 85 Nm. Tool setter calibration ±0.002 mm.
SEMI-ANNUAL: Spindle bearing inspection per Okuma SB-2023-07, geometric accuracy per ISO 230-2, ball screw backlash compensation, electrical cabinet thermal scan.

============================================================
3. PM SCHEDULES — CNC MILLS (equip-cnc-mill-01), (equip-cnc-mill-02)
============================================================

DAILY: Spindle warm-up (15 min, 2000 RPM), tool magazine check, coolant flow (15 LPM DMG / 14 LPM Haas).
WEEKLY: Z-axis ball screw lube (Mobilgrease XHP 222), ATC finger inspection, spindle taper blue test (>80% contact).
MONTHLY: Vibration baseline 1.5 mm/s for (equip-cnc-mill-01), 1.7 mm/s for (equip-cnc-mill-02). Alert: 3.5 mm/s. Alarm: 6.0 mm/s. Coolant flush and biocide treatment.
SEMI-ANNUAL: Drawbar force test (min 12 kN BT40), ball bar test per ISO 10791-6.

============================================================
4. PM SCHEDULES — GRINDER & WELDING ROBOT
============================================================

(equip-cnc-grind-01) Studer S33:
DAILY: Dress wheel every 50 parts or on SPC drift. Coolant flow 18 LPM.
WEEKLY: Hydrostatic oil level, wheel balance (<0.5 μm).
MONTHLY: Vibration baseline 1.2 mm/s, alert 2.5, alarm 4.5. Roundness test <0.3 μm.

(equip-weld-rob-01) Fanuc ARC Mate 120iD:
DAILY: Torch tip/nozzle inspection, wire feed tension, gas flow (18-22 L/min MIG), TCP verification.
WEEKLY: Lubricate 6 axes (Molywhite RE No.00), cable dress inspection, fume extraction check.
MONTHLY: Vibration baseline 0.8 mm/s, alert 1.5, alarm 2.5. Re-teach 3 reference points (max 0.15 mm deviation). Replace torch liner and contact tip.
SEMI-ANNUAL: Joint backlash J1-J6, wire feeder roll replacement, servo current analysis.
