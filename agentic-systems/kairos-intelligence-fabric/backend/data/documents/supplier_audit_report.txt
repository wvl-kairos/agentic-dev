2. AUDIT FINDINGS

- Calibration program: All measurement equipment within calibration. Good traceability.
- Nonconformance tracking: Using digital NCR system. Average closure time: 12 days (target: 10 days).

2.2 Process Control (Score: 78/100)
- Heat treatment: Furnace temperature uniformity survey overdue by 2 months.
- Material traceability: Full lot traceability from raw material to shipped product. Bar coding system effective.
- In-process inspection: SPC charts maintained for key dimensions. Cp values generally above 1.33.
- MAJOR FINDING: Surface grinding process lacks automated in-process gauging. Relying on operator checks every 25th part.

2.3 Delivery Performance (Score: 72/100)
- 6% late deliveries in Q4 2025 due to capacity constraints.
- Lead time increased from 4 weeks to 6 weeks for custom forgings.
- Supplier adding second shift to address capacity. Expected improvement by Q2 2026.
