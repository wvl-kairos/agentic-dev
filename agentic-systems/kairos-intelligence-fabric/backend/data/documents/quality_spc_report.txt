EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
This report analyzes SPC data for the injection molding production line (line-injection) processing products PROD-004 (Automotive Bracket Assembly) and PROD-005 (Industrial Valve Housing).

1. PROCESS CAPABILITY ANALYSIS

Product: Automotive Bracket Assembly (prod-bracket-assy)
- Critical dimension: Wall thickness (spec: 3.20 ± 0.15 mm)
- Cp = 1.45, Cpk = 1.32
- Process is capable and centered.
- X-bar chart: No out-of-control signals detected.
- R chart: Range stable, average range = 0.08 mm.

Product: Industrial Valve Housing (prod-valve-housing)
- Critical dimension: Bore diameter (spec: 25.00 ± 0.05 mm)
- Cp = 1.12, Cpk = 0.98
- WARNING: Cpk below 1.0 threshold. Process drift detected.
- X-bar chart: Western Electric Rule 2 violation on Jan 15-17 (2 of 3 consecutive points beyond 2σ).
- Root cause: Material lot variation from supplier SUPP-003 (Advanced Polymers Inc).
- Corrective action: Incoming inspection tightened for polymer lots. Supplier notified.
