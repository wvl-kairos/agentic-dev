VENDOR QUALITY AGREEMENT
Document ID: DOC-VQA-001
Effective Date: 2026-01-01
Between: Cerebro Manufacturing Inc. and Key Suppliers

1. PARTIES AND SCOPE

This agreement applies to the following approved suppliers:
- Precision Steel Co (supp-precision-steel) — Steel billets, bar stock, forgings
- Advanced Polymers Inc (supp-advanced-polymers) — Nylon 66, PPS resin compounds
- Global Components Ltd (supp-global-components) — Bearings, seals, inserts, fasteners
- ChemTech Solutions (supp-chemtech) — Coatings, lubricants, chemical compounds
- TitanParts Inc (supp-titanparts) — Titanium hardware, specialty alloys

2. QUALITY REQUIREMENTS

2.1 Incoming Material Specifications
- All materials must conform to purchase order specifications and referenced standards.
- Certificate of Conformance (CoC) required with every shipment.
- Material test reports (MTR) required for all metal raw materials.
- Shelf-life materials: Minimum 75% remaining life at time of delivery.

2.2 Defect Rate Limits
| Supplier | Current PPM | Target PPM | Max Acceptable |
|----------|-------------|------------|----------------|
| Precision Steel Co | 850 | 500 | 1,000 |
| Advanced Polymers Inc | 1,200 | 800 | 1,500 |
| Global Components Ltd | 320 | 250 | 500 |
| ChemTech Solutions | 150 | 100 | 300 |
| TitanParts Inc | 95 | 50 | 200 |

2.3 Delivery Requirements
- On-time delivery target: 97%
- Advance Ship Notice (ASN) required 48 hours before delivery.
- No partial shipments without prior approval.
- Emergency orders: 72-hour expedite capability required.

3. AUDIT AND ASSESSMENT
- Annual quality audit for Tier 1 suppliers (spend > $500K/year).
- Biennial audit for Tier 2 suppliers.
- Right-of-access clause: Cerebro may audit supplier facilities with 5 business days notice.
- Supplier scorecard reviewed quarterly: Quality (40%), Delivery (30%), Cost (20%), Service (10%).

4. CORRECTIVE ACTION PROCESS
- Supplier NCR response required within 5 business days.
- 8D report required for critical defects or recurring issues.
- Corrective action verification within 30 days.
- Three unresolved CARs within 12 months triggers supplier review and potential disqualification.

5. CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT
- Annual cost reduction target: 2-3% through process improvements.
- Joint Kaizen events: Minimum 1 per year for Tier 1 suppliers.
- Technology sharing: Quarterly business reviews to discuss new capabilities.

6. SUPPLIER RATINGS (CURRENT)
- Precision Steel Co: 4.2/5.0 (Good — capacity concerns)
- Advanced Polymers Inc: 3.8/5.0 (Acceptable — material consistency issues)
- Global Components Ltd: 4.5/5.0 (Excellent)
- ChemTech Solutions: 4.7/5.0 (Excellent)
- TitanParts Inc: 4.6/5.0 (Excellent)
